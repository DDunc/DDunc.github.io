Learning Projects Awaiting First Commit

RegEx/JavaScript/Separation of Concerns/Modularity
Germanizer is a work in progress with the goal of being able to recreate most (if not all) of the historical vowel and consonant shifts between english and german pronunciation/orthography. Because English and German are part of the same West Germanic language family, this tool could help language learners identify non-obvious English/German cognates and rapidly expand their active vocabulary. There are a number of technical challenges to making it do more than produce funny sounding faux-German, but that may have its uses too.

This project is serving as my continuing introduction to regular expressions, regular expression methods in JavaScript, and for thinking about how to manage complexity.
